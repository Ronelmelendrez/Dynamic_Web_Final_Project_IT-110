### NASA API - Why This Choice?

**Selected APIs:**
1. **Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD)**
2. **Mars Rover Photos**
3. **EPIC (Earth Polychromatic Imaging Camera)**

**Rationale:**
- âœ… **Visual Impact**: Stunning, high-quality imagery perfect for marketing
- âœ… **Emotional Resonance**: Space exploration inspires universal wonder
- âœ… **Data Richness**: Multiple endpoints for varied storytelling
- âœ… **Accessibility**: Free, well-documented, reliable API
- âœ… **Brand Association**: NASA's credibility and inspiration
- âœ… **Marketing Angle**: Perfect for "exploration" and "discovery" themes

**Rejected Alternatives:**

- Art Institute API: Limited emotional range, static content
- Weather APIs: Too utilitarian, lacks inspirational quality

**Marketing Message**: *This journey began long ago, and you're part of it*
